8 ounces Whole Butter
1 ounce Ground Garlic
three quarters cup Breadcrumbs
three quarters cup Grated Parmesan Cheese
1 TBSP Salt
1 and a half pounds Ground Beef
One-third cup Parsley, chopped
3 Large Eggs
Cooking Instructions:
1. Place butter in sauce pan and cook until melted; remove from heat and allow to cool.
2. Combine garlic, breadcrumbs, cheese, and salt in large mixing bowl and mix thoroughly.
3. Add the beef, parsley, eggs and cooled butter to the breadcrumb mixture.
4. Mix well by hand until all ingredients are evenly distributed.
5. Form into 6 ounce balls and place on sheet pan.
6. Bake in pre-heated 350-degree oven for 25 minutes.
